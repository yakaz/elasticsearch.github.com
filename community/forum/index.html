ElasticSearch User is where ElasticSearch users come to seek help, announce projects, and discuss all kind of matters surrounding the framework and the community.

You need to register with your google account to the list. At the bottom left side pane, enter your google account user (email) continue with the login process. Below you can find an embedded nabble mirror of the mailing list. You still need to register to the google groups mailing list in order to post.

Below is a nabble mirror of the forum. Nabble seems to not always post messages to the mailing list, so make sure to post directly through google groups.
